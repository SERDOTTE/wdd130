1. Overview

1.1 Purpose

The purpose of a rafting company's online presence is to increase visibility, attract customers, facilitate bookings and build trust through detailed information, customer connection and effective marketing.

1.2 Audience

Our rafting company's target customers are families looking for outdoor activities and corporate groups looking for team building events, who want an exciting and safe rafting experience. There must be clear information about the types of tours offered, prices, availability and security policies. Have photos and videos that show the excitement of rafting trips, testimonials from satisfied customers, information about equipment provided and what they should bring with them. Your needs include detailed information about the rafting routes, water quality and the experience of the guides. They will likely access the site primarily through mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets, especially while on the go or planning their outdoor adventures.
