Trips

Beginners

- a calm and safe route for a light adventure.
- participants can enjoy the surrounding nature, lots of fun for all ages.
- trained staff to ensure safety and provide an educational experience about rafting and the aquatic environment.

Intermediate

- higher current level, providing more excitement
- perfect for groups of friends, a more intense adventure, but with a difficulty still accessible to brave beginners.
- participants receive detailed instructions to ensure safety and fun.

Advanced

- with rough waters and technical challenges.
- the rapids are faster and more unpredictable, providing an adventure that demands strength, endurance and coordination from the team.
- guides with extensive experience in fast waters accompany the group, a safe and exciting experience for everyone.

Trip's Observations
Beginner Calm and safe route, suitable for families and children.
Intermediate Higher currents, moderate excitement, ideal for adults.
Advanced Rough waters and technical challenges, for experienced rafters.
